FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our wholesale services

What are your minimum order quantities (MOQs)?

MOQs vary by product format and packaging. Bulk tea typically requires a 500kg minimum, while tea bags start at 50,000 units.

What are your typical lead times?

Standard production lead time is 4–6 weeks from order confirmation. Shipping time varies by destination.

Do you offer custom packaging and private labeling?

Yes, we provide complete private label solutions including custom blends, packaging design, and branding.

What are your shipping terms and payment options?

We offer standard trade terms including FOB, CIF, and DDP depending on your destination. Payment terms are discussed during the enquiry process, with flexible options available for established partners.

Can you provide samples before bulk orders?

Absolutely. Samples are available for evaluation and are usually credited against your first order.

Which markets do you currently export to?

We export to over 40 countries across North America, Europe, Middle East, Asia-Pacific, and Africa. Our team is experienced with diverse regulatory requirements.
