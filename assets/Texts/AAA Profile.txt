Traditional Craftsmanship
Sri Lanka remains globally respected for its orthodox tea manufacturing process and traditional hand-plucking methods — selecting only the finest two leaves and a bud. This meticulous approach preserves leaf integrity and cup character.
Ozone Friendly & Ethically Produced
Sri Lanka is recognized for producing ozone-friendly tea, cultivated without methyl bromide fumigation. This reinforces Ceylon Tea’s standing as a responsibly produced agricultural commodity.
The Lion Logo – A Mark of Authenticity
The Lion Logo, issued and regulated by the Sri Lanka Tea Board, is an internationally recognized symbol of quality and origin authenticity.
Only teas that are:
	•	100% Pure Ceylon Tea
	•	Packed in Sri Lanka
	•	Meeting strict quality standards
are permitted to bear this mark.
It is more than a logo — it is a guarantee.
