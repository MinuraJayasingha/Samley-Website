Heritage & Global Positioning

Why Ceylon Tea Stands Apart

Sri Lanka, globally renowned as the home of Ceylon Tea, offers an unparalleled terroir shaped by elevation, climate, and artisanal expertise. From high-grown brisk liquors to low-grown bold liquors, Samley carefully sources and curates teas that represent the full spectrum of Sri Lanka's tea heritage.

Arguably the Finest Tea in the World

Ceylon Tea has earned its global reputation through an uncompromising commitment to quality, origin integrity, and artisanal tradition. It is not merely a beverage — it is a geographically distinctive product shaped by nature and mastery.

A Terroir That Cannot Be Replicated

Grown between 3,000–8,000 ft above sea level, Sri Lanka's highland climate produces a brightness and complexity found nowhere else on earth. Each region — Nuwara Eliya, Dimbula, Uva — imparts its own unmistakable character.

Traditional Craftsmanship

Sri Lanka remains globally respected for its orthodox tea manufacturing process and traditional hand-plucking methods — selecting only the finest two leaves and a bud. This meticulous approach preserves leaf integrity and cup character.

Ozone Friendly & Ethically Produced

Sri Lanka is recognized for producing ozone-friendly tea, cultivated without methyl bromide fumigation. This reinforces Ceylon Tea's standing as a responsibly produced agricultural commodity aligned with global environmental standards.

The Lion Logo — A Mark of Authenticity

The Lion Logo, issued and regulated by the Sri Lanka Tea Board, is an internationally recognized symbol of quality and origin authenticity. Only teas that are 100% Pure Ceylon Tea, packed in Sri Lanka, and meeting strict quality standards are permitted to bear this mark. It is more than a logo — it is a guarantee.

The Samley Loyalty Pledge — CSR Commitment

One Percent of Every Sale,
Reinvested in the World We Share.

1% of sales revenue generated from each Samley product sold is allocated to a dedicated CSR Fund managed by appointed trustees. This fund is exclusively committed to supporting the education of children from tea plantation worker families, assisting differently-abled fellow Sri Lankans, and creating access to resources that enable dignity and independence.

We believe commercial success must translate into measurable social impact. Every cup contributes to a greater cause.
