Black Tea with Cinnamon  - SPICY, BOLD & AROMATIC

Ingredients – Black Tea & Cinnamon Flavoring 
Grade – BOPF- High Grown 

Brewing Temperature- 90-100C & 4-5min


Liquor: Bright, deep amber with excellent clarity — a hallmark of high-grown BOPF teas.
Aroma: Warm, sweet spice notes of Ceylon cinnamon rise prominently, layered over a brisk and lively black tea base.
Flavour profile: A bold, full-bodied Ceylon tea foundation complemented by a smooth, naturally sweet cinnamon spice. The spice is pronounced yet harmoniously balanced, offering warmth without overpowering the palate.
Finish: Clean, warming, and subtly sweet with a lingering cinnamon glow.

BOP1 - RICH, DEEP & GOLDEN

The leaves, being clean, short-wiry, and dark, are consistent with the typical characteristics of low-elevation tea. The resulting tea displays a deep golden-orange colour, along with a malt-infused aroma, underpinned by subtle raw undertones.
Ingredients – Black Tea 
Grade – BOP1- Low Grown 

Brewing Temperature- 90-100C & 4-5min
Liquor: Deep golden-orange to burnished copper with strong colour intensity and excellent brightness.
Aroma: Rich and malty with gentle caramelised notes, layered over subtle earthy undertones distinctive of low-grown teas.
Flavour profile: Full-bodied and robust with pronounced maltiness. Hints of dark honey and light cocoa nuances emerge as the cup develops, balanced by mild raw undertones that add complexity.
Finish: Long, warm, and lingering with a mellow sweetness and structured depth.

Golden Tips - Delicate & Floral 

Exquisite Golden Tips tea is crafted from meticulously selected single buds plucked from a unique tea bush variety cultivated in high-altitude hills. These delicate buds, adorned with a glistening sheen that transforms into a golden hue during the tea leaves' drying process, give this tea its distinguished name Golden Tips. This premium offering, Golden Tips, is a favourite among connoisseurs, offering an aromatic and flavourful tea experience that's best enjoyed without any additives. Its distinctively smooth and refined character makes it a delightful beverage suitable for any time of the day.
Ingredients – White Tea 
Grade – Golden Tips- Low Grown 
Brewing Temperature- 90C-100C & 2-3min
Liquor: Pale gold to light amber, exceptionally clear
Aroma: Sweet floral honey, soft malt, delicate fruit
Flavour Profile: Light to medium-light body, silky & smooth, subtle notes of honey, ripe peach, and light caramel
Finish: Clean, lingering sweetness with refined elegance


Silver Tips- Delicate & Floral 

The gentle and slightly sweet Silver Tips tea produces an exceptionally pure tea experience. Silver Tips tea undergoes minimal processing, which preserves a heightened concentration of antioxidants. These Silver Tips are the uppermost buds, characterized by their unopened, delicate leaves covered in a fine silver fuzz. This extraordinary pale-yellow infusion is best enjoyed without any additives and can even be re-steeped for a second cup.
Ingredients – White Tea 
Grade – Golden Tips- Low Grown 
Brewing Temperature- 90C-100C & 2-3min

Liquor: Very pale champagne gold, crystal clear
Aroma: Delicate floral sweetness, light cucumber, fresh meadow
Flavour Profile: Very light body, Notes of honeydew, white peach, and soft floral nectar
Finish: Silky, almost weightless, Clean, gently sweet, lingering elegance
