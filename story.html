Story of Samley

Samley Teas began with a simple idea — to share the rich taste and timeless charm of Ceylon Tea with the world. Known for its bold flavor and smooth finish, Ceylon Tea is more than just a drink; it's an experience that connects people across cultures.

From the beginning, we have focused on doing things with care and purpose. At Samley Teas, it's not just about selling tea — it's about offering something real, something memorable. Every blend is created with quality in mind, designed to meet the tastes of tea lovers everywhere.

We believe in building trust through consistency, strong values, and thoughtful choices. The Lion Logo on our packs stands as a symbol of authenticity — a promise that what you're enjoying is true Ceylon Tea.

Today, Samley Teas is part of tea moments in over 25 countries. And while we're always moving forward, our heart stays grounded in one simple goal: to keep the spirit of Ceylon Tea alive, one cup at a time.

Company Profile

Who We
Are.

Samley Teas (Pvt) Ltd is a Sri Lankan tea manufacturer and exporter committed to delivering the world's finest Ceylon Tea under strict quality standards. We operate across the full value chain — from sourcing single-origin leaf at altitude to blending, packaging, and distributing finished products globally.


Our portfolio spans premium loose-leaf teas, biodegradable tea bags, wellness blends, and bespoke gift collections — all traceable to certified, ethical origins. We supply retail chains, hospitality brands, specialty tea importers, and direct-to-consumer channels across Europe, the Middle East, Asia, and beyond.


Samley holds international quality certifications and operates under a governance framework designed to protect workers, smallholder farmers, and the natural ecosystems that make Ceylon Tea possible.

The Leaf Behind the Legend

Why Ceylon Tea Stands Apart

Unique Terroir

Grown between 3,000–8,000 ft above sea level, Sri Lanka's highland climate produces a brightness and complexity found nowhere else on earth.

Protected Origin

The Lion Logo is a government-controlled mark of authenticity — only pure Ceylon Tea, grown and packed in Sri Lanka, can carry it.

Rich in Antioxidants

High-grown black teas carry exceptional levels of polyphenols and flavonoids — compounds linked to cardiovascular health and immunity support.

Naturally Pure

Ceylon tea is grown in an island nation with no land borders — a natural barrier that supports cleaner cultivation with minimal contamination risk.

World-Class Flavour

From the muscatel notes of Nuwara Eliya to the full-bodied richness of Sabaragamuwa — every region offers a distinctive, award-winning cup.

What Guides Us

Three Core Values.

Ethics & Compliance

We operate with full transparency across our supply chain. From fair labour standards on the estate to honest labelling on the shelf, ethics is not a policy — it's a practice. We maintain independent audits, worker grievance channels, and zero tolerance for exploitation at any tier of our business.

Mastery

Ceylon Tea is one of the world's most complex agricultural products. We respect that complexity. Our team includes certified tea tasters, experienced blenders, and quality specialists who obsess over every detail — from moisture content at intake to infusion clarity in the final brew. We chase mastery, not shortcuts.

Environment

The highlands that produce Ceylon Tea are among Sri Lanka's most ecologically sensitive landscapes. We work with estates that practice shade-grown cultivation, water-smart irrigation, and soil-regenerative farming. Our packaging transition to biodegradable materials is ongoing — and our carbon footprint reduction targets are on the record.
