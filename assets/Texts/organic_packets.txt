A curated collection of indulgent tea blends, where fine Ceylon teas meet carefully selected fruits, spices, and botanicals. Each blend is masterfully balanced and packed in luxury tea bags to preserve aroma, flavour, and freshness—delivering a sophisticated tea experience in every cup.

Chocolate Delight
A refreshing harmony of rich cocoa nibs and cool peppermint leaves, beautifully layered over fine black tea. The depth of the tea provides structure and warmth, while the gentle chocolate notes of roasted cocoa are lifted by the bright, invigorating character of peppermint.
95-100C & 4-5min
Ingredients: Cocoa, Peppermint, Ceylon Black Tea -FBOP- Low Grown 
Liquor: Deep amber infusion with bright clarity.
Aroma: Inviting notes of roasted cocoa layered with cool, refreshing peppermint.
Flavour Profile: A harmonious balance of robust black tea depth, gentle chocolate nuances from cocoa nibs, and the lively freshness of peppermint.
Finish: Smooth and refreshing with lingering cocoa warmth and a cooling minty aftertaste.
Hibiscus Tonic 
An elegant botanical infusion uniting the calming depth of holy basil, the vibrant tang of hibiscus, and the delicate floral sweetness of rose petals — beautifully layered over fine black tea. The richness of the tea adds body and structure, creating a harmonious cup that is aromatic, gently bright, and gracefully balanced.
95-100C & 4-5min
 Ingredients: Hhibiscus,Holy Basil,Rose Petal, Ceylon Black Tea -FBOP- Low Grown 
Liquor: Bright deep amber infusion with subtle ruby highlights from hibiscus.
Aroma: Fresh and aromatic with floral rose notes, gentle herbal nuances of holy basil, and lively hints of hibiscus.
Flavour Profile: Rich black tea character harmoniously balanced with tangy hibiscus, soft floral sweetness from rose petals, and mild herbal depth from holy basil.
Finish: Clean, vibrant, and lightly floral with a refreshing tangy aftertaste.
Apple Sensation
Crisp apple sweetness harmonised with warming cinnamon and smooth green tea. Refreshing yet comforting, this blend is a timeless classic with a refined, modern character.
70-80C & 1-2min
Ingredients: Apple, Cinnamon, Ceylon Green Tea -GP1- High & Medium 

Liquor: Clear light golden infusion with bright clarity.
Aroma: Fresh and inviting with crisp apple notes complemented by warm hints of cinnamon.
Flavour Profile: Soft vegetal notes of green tea harmoniously balanced with natural apple sweetness and gentle warming spice from cinnamon.
Finish: Clean, lightly sweet, and comforting with a subtle lingering spice.

Spicy Orange
A bold and invigorating blend combining zesty orange with a complex medley of warming spices and herbs. Layered, aromatic, and deeply satisfying, this tea awakens the senses with every sip.
70-80C & 1-2min
Ingredients: Orange Peel, Cinnamon Leaf, Cinnamon, Ginger, Clove, Black Pepper, Curry Leaves, Fennel Seed, Ceylon Green Tea GP1 -High & medium 
Liquor: Bright amber infusion with a warm, inviting glow.
Aroma: Zesty orange notes intertwined with fragrant warming spices and gentle herbal undertones.
Flavour Profile: Lively citrus brightness balanced with a complex medley of warming spices and aromatic herbs, creating a bold and invigorating cup.
Finish: Warm, vibrant, and lingering with refreshing citrus and subtle spice notes.

Tangy Passion
A vibrant fusion of fine green tea layered with rosehip, apple, lemon peel, lemongrass, marigold petals, got kola, guava, and passionfruit peel. This lively composition brings together gentle vegetal notes of green tea with bright citrus accents and soft tropical sweetness.
70-80C & 1-2min
 Ingredients: Rosehip , Apple , Lemon peel , Lemongrass, Merigold , Gotu Kola, Guava, Passionfruit peel , Ceylon Green Tea GP1 High & Medium 
Liquor: Bright light golden infusion with lively clarity.
Aroma: Fresh and uplifting with citrusy lemon notes, tropical hints of passionfruit and guava, and gentle herbal nuances.
Flavour Profile: Soft vegetal notes of green tea harmonised with tangy rosehip, bright citrus accents, and subtle tropical sweetness from passionfruit and guava.
Finish: Clean, vibrant, and refreshing with lingering fruity and citrus undertones.
